About Team Raspberry

Team Raspberry is a group of highly dedicated and skilled individuals committed to creating innovative solutions that combine technology and creativity. With expertise in hardware integration, artificial intelligence, UX/UI design, and rigorous testing, our team ensures that every aspect of our robot project is carefully planned and executed. We pride ourselves on collaboration, innovation, and a shared passion for making a meaningful impact in the field of robotics.

Our project revolves around building a robust, interactive, and intelligent desk robot designed to assist users with daily tasks and educational activities. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies and the diverse skills of each team member, Team Raspberry has embraced a unified vision: to push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of personal robotics.
